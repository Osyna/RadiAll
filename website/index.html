FEATURES

Everything on the wheel

One overlay, three modes, and it never touches the rest of your setup.

Three rings, three keys

Super + A your apps, Super + W open windows, Super + D the focused window's actions. Each ring on its own shortcut.

A real tray icon

A proper StatusNotifierItem in your bar — open any ring or the settings straight from the tray.

Window switcher

Every open window, grouped by app, with live thumbnails as you scroll a multi-window app.

Focus actions

Close, float, fullscreen, .desktop actions and your own custom shortcuts — arranged around the app's icon.

Follow the cursor

Flick the mouse and the accent sector tracks it anywhere on screen — no need to land on the ring.

Tune it in-app

Colours, sizes, shortcuts and behaviour — all in a built-in settings panel. No config files to edit.
